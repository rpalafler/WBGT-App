************************************
FOLDER: hooks
************************************
DESCRIPTION:
This folder contains custom React hooks to encapsulate 
reusable logic, such as managing state, effects, or 
interactions used across the app.

EXAMPLES:
- useFetchWeather.js
  Fetches weather data from an API.
- useToggle.js
  Manages "on/off" states like toggling modals or menus.
